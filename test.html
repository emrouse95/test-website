Welcome to our book club! We are a small group of women who meet up once a month to discuss the book we chose for the previous month as well as choose our next book. We are open to a wide variety of genres including but not limited to: Fantasy, Crime, Drama, Thriller, etc. We all love to read but most importantly, we love being able to support each other!

FAQ
  • Q. How do you pick the books?

    A. We each submit a book that we think the group would like and then we randomly draw from thos options.

    Q. What days of the week do you meet?

    A. Typically we meet on the weekend since that is when everyone has a free day to get together.

    Q. What happens if I fall behind or don't read the chosen books?

    A. We understand life happens! Please, do not feel like you are excluded from any get-togethers for not reading the book. Whether you read it all, read part of it, or simply don't finish a book, we welcome you to still join any and all meetings you woud like!
